**Background**: Natural cells rely on a cytoskeleton to reorganize and divide, which has been a challenge for synthetic biologists. Adamala bypassed this by using protein tags to crowd and bend the membrane, triggering division. The work was initially rejected by Cell but has since been published on bioRxiv and submitted to a new journal.
